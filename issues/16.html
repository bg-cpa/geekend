Remembering our heroes

Welcome back, friends, to the Geekend! It's Memorial Day weekend, and that means there's no shortage of fun things to do. It's easy to get swept up in the excitement of a long weekend (and an HD remake of Dragon Quest III), but take a moment to thank those who made the ultimate sacrifice for our country. They are the real heroes we should thank for the amazing nerdy lives we have.

 

Whether you're in the pool, on the road, or simply relaxing at home, we at the Geekend wish you a happy and refreshing Memorial Day weekend!
