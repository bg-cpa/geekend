Happy National Space Day!!

Welcome to the Geekend! It’s National Space Day, and that means we’re celebrating all things out of this world (literally)!!


From the fantastic to the intergalactic, Huntsville loves space! Take a moment today to appreciate all the amazing work we’ve done over the past few decades to explore the vast expanse of outer space.


Once thought to be beyond our reach and understanding, we can now share photos from trillions of miles away with just a few clicks. That’s pretty dang awesome!


So grab a telescope, and take a moment to appreciate all things space today as you kick off your GEEKend!!
