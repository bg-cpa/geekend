Halo Infinite technical preview begins!

Welcome back Geekend fam! We’ve made it to another Friday, and it’s a big one for the gamers out there!


This week kicks off the first Halo Infinite technical test, giving us the first ever gameplay look at the next entry in the Halo franchise. This techicial test is intened to experiment with server load and resolve potential issues, but it’s also our first look at actual Halo Infinite gameplay.


The only bummer, the technical test is closed, so it’s invite only for now. However, there are no restrictions on streaming, so you can get your first look at Halo Infinite multiplayer gameplay over on Twitch all weekend long!
