A new national holiday approaches!

Welcome back to the Geekend!! This weekend marks a very special milestone for our nation as we now officially acknowledge Juneteenth as our newest national holiday!!


If you don’t know what Juneteenth is, no worries, class is in session. Juneteenth celebrates the emancipation of African American slaves in the United States. Specifically, it commemorates the anniversary of June 19th, 1865, when Union General Gordon Granger proclaimed freedom for all slaves in Texas. This move was the most impactful enforcement of the Emancipation Proclamation, until the ratification of the 13th Amendment later that year.

 

We encourage everyone to learn more about Juneteenth and embrace this new holiday as an opportunity to celebrate African American freedom in the United States!
