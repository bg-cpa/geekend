Meet Ink & Lyre, a Dungeons & Dragons content creator here in Huntsville!

Welcome back Geekend squad! This week we’re excited to share a new project from a fantastic group of Huntsville local D&D content publishers, Ink & Lyre!


They are getting ready to launch their first Kickstarter for their new D&D supplement, Stargazer’s Guide to Aroria, introducing a full range of character options and campaign settings based on the zodiac!
We’re excited to share this new homebrew sourcebook with the Geekend audience to highlight Ink & Lyre's hard work publishing unique D&D content right here in Huntsville!


Their Kickstarter campaign starts on Monday, July 19th, so mark your calendars and help support our local creators at Ink & Lyre!

 


Ink & Lyre features their unique zodiac-centric campaign setting and character options in a weekly campaign stream on Twitch, Fate’s End. See characters from Stargazer’s Guide to Aroria in action each Monday at 7:30 PM on https://www.twitch.tv/inkandlyre.

Follow the Kickstarter!
